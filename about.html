ABOUT

BobTheDreiz is a Hanoi-born producer/rapper who starts releasing his own music around June 2020 and his main genre is Trap, Dubstep, Hybrid Trap, House. Later in 2022, BobTheDreiz started working with COIN, his highschool friend, formed the duo BobTheCoin and started making rap production. Both COIN and BobTheDreiz are college students studying in the US. Currently, COIN is the Boston-based leading rapper of the duo and BobTheDreiz is the upstate New York-based producer / rapper in all BobTheCoin's singles.
